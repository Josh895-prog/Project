About Us

Our Mission

Empowering Communities

Dunia Bora is a collective of scientists, volunteers, and digital activists dedicated to planetary restoration. We believe that conservation shouldn't be passive—it requires immediate, coordinated physical and technical action.

By coupling state-of-the-art geo-monitoring tools with local conservation groups, we deliver measurable, high-impact results directly to the areas that need it most.
